# Business Role — Vision Completion Gate

Runs once every question under `## Business Questions` in `aidlc-discovery-state.md` is marked `[x]` (or the user has explicitly waived remaining questions).

---

## Step 1 — Run the open-questions collector

Execute `shared/open-questions-collector.md` for the Business role only. This produces or updates `Product-Definition/open-questions.md`.

## Step 2 — Render the Vision Document

Read:
- `Product-Definition/interview/role-selection.md`
- `Product-Definition/interview/project-type.md`
- `Product-Definition/interview/business/vision-answers-history.md`
- `Product-Definition/open-questions.md`

Write `Product-Definition/vision-document.md` using the structure below. All sections are mandatory. If a section has no content, include a short "Not applicable — <reason>" line rather than omitting the heading.

## Step 3 — Validate the rendered document

Apply all checks in `common/content-validation.md`. Specifically:

- All section headings present.
- Tables have complete rows (no empty required columns).
- Success metrics are measurable (have a target and a method).
- Excluded features are not silently missing — if the table is empty, the document should say so explicitly.

If any check fails, do not present the gate. Instead, ask the user to revisit the offending section before proceeding.

## Step 4 — Present the completion gate

Display exactly two options (no emergent third option):

```
Vision Document complete — review before we continue.

  Product-Definition/vision-document.md is ready for your review.

Your options:

  1) Request Changes — tell me what to fix and I'll update the document
     (and back-propagate to vision-answers-history.md and aidlc-discovery-state.md).

  2) Approve and Continue — mark Business as ✅ complete and
     (if your role selection is "Both (sequential)") move on to the
     Technical interview.

What would you like to do?
```

Wait for the user's explicit choice. Do NOT proceed to Technical on your own.

## Step 5 — Handle the user's choice

- **Request Changes** → ask where specifically, update the relevant `*-answers-history.md` (append a correction entry, never overwrite), re-render the document, re-run validation, re-present the gate.
- **Approve and Continue** →
  1. Update `aidlc-discovery-state.md`:
     ```markdown
     ## Role Progress
     - Business (Vision Document):  ✅ Complete (approved <ISO8601>)
     ```
  2. Log the approval in `audit.md` with stage label `Business Interview — Completion`.
  3. Check the session's role selection:
     - `Business` only → proceed to Final Handoff (see `aidlc-discovery-core-workflow.md`).
     - `Both (sequential)` → proceed to `technical/tech-env-interview.md`.
     - `Technical` only → should not happen at this gate; log a warning.

## Step 6 — Log everything

Every interaction in this gate is logged per `common/audit-format.md`. This includes the gate presentation, the user's choice, any change requests, and the final approval.
